### Quick interpretation

- **Days 0–2**: High friction, high drop-off (most users bail here if they expected “Linux but secure”).
- **Week 1**: Turning point — first “reboot feels good” moment.
- **Month 1+**: Regret curve inverts — they start hating their old systems instead.
- **Year 1+**: The system disappears. Users forget it’s even special. That’s when you’ve won.

This table is the real adoption predictor — not features, not benchmarks, not threat models.  
It shows the emotional timeline most people actually go through.
